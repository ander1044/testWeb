About

Who are we?



The Luncedo Foundation’s structure of projects are focused on creating a positive impact in the community by identifying health, personal care needs, hygiene and education for underprivileged children as we believe that the future of the world is in the hands of the upcoming generation. Furthermore, children are often the most vulnerable and susceptible to the negative effects of being underprivileged therefore we see fit to focus on their development, personal well-being and need for resources. Although the foundation mainly focuses on children it also caters for the elderly at old age homes and the homeless.

Mission



To create a culture of giving back where there is none and contribute to the development of the community.

Vision


To assist the underprivileged and vulnerable through projects that will provide necessities such as school stationery, books, food and tutoring.
